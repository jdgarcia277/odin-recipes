Barbacoa

Delicious barbacoa

An easy crockpot meal that will always impress the family

Ingredients:

  • Beef Cheek
  • Yellow Onion
  • Garlic
  • Bay Leaf
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Rinse beef cheek and set in crockpot
  2. Cut onion in half and place in crockpot
  3. Add two to three cloves of garlic
  4. Add bayleaf
  5. Generously sprinkle salt
  6. Cook on high for 8 hours, or low for 12
