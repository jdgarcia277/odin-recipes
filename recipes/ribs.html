Oven-baked Ribs

Easy oven baked ribs that are tender and have the flavor to keep people asking for more

Ingredients:

  • Ribs
  • Rub of your choice

Steps:

  1. Cut off back membrane (helps improve tenderness)
  2. Generously apply rub to both sides
  3. Cover ribs in foil and place bone side down for 4 hours at 275 degrees
  4. Optional: Last hour apply bbq sauce
