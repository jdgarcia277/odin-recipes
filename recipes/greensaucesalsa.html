Green Sauce Salsa

"Kasper Killer" Salsa

A chunky warm jalapeno salsa that will add a kick to any meal

Ingredients:

  • 6 large fresh Jalapenos
  • 1/4 large Yellow Onion
  • 2 large Garlic Cloves
  • 1/2-3/4 cup Corn Oil
  • 1 tsp. of Kosher Salt

Steps:

  1. Bring small pot of water to boil. Simmer the jalapenos and onions for about 15-20 minutes until fork tender. Keep some of the excess water
  2. Combine corn oil, garlic, jalapenos, onion and salt in blender
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy, about 3-6 minutes.
  4. Taste for salt, and adjust if necessary. Transfer to squeeze bottle and refigerate. Lasts about a week.
